About Whitney Houston

Known as one of the greatest vocalists to have ever lived, it's rare to overhear any serious discussion about singers without a mention of Whitney. Whitney captivated audiences around the world with not only her highly intensive and skilled vocal performances, but with her charismatic personality that was often displayed during her performances. She set the bar so high for what a vocalist can do and was the embodiment of the voice being an instrument in it's own right. My favorite vocal skill of her's is definitely the color she adds to her singing via riffs and runs. She does them with excellence and in seemingly perfect timing. As a result, one simply cannot help but remain engaged in her performance.
